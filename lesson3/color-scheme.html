Choosing My Colors

When deciding on what colors I wanted to use, I wanted to capture the theme of Wandavision. These colors are similar to the color scheme used in Wandavision. I also wanted to choose colors that feel magical like Wandavision, but make it more professional. These colors will work well with my website because they give off a fun vibe to them. I want viewers to see that Westview is a fun place to visit.
