Typography

I chose three different font families: Varela Round, Arvo, and Rokkitt. I wanted the font style to be different from the headers, paragraphs, and the footer to make the page stand out more. I chose these styles because they don't have a crazy texture to them and it captures the readers attention. I used these fonts all over my siteplan as an example of what the text looks like. I used Varela Round for the headers, Rokkit for the paragraphs, and Arvo for the footer and the navigation text.
