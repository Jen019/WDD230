Welcome to Temple Inn & Suites!!!

Temple Inn & Suites was built to help bring the joy of spending time in the Temple to those who travel near and far to partake in the blessings of a Temple. Our Suites are fully equipped to accomodate every traveler's needs to relieve the ease of travel to focus on the journey to the Temple. On your vacation you can walk around the grounds of the hotel gardens and fountains to enjoy time with family and friends. Be sure to stop by our Genealogy Center and find help to find names to take to the Temple. Kids who are unable to attend the Temple can have a great time in our game and daycare center ran by our staff. Don't know a great Wedding venue? Look no further because we have a beautiful reception hall to celebrate the magic and happiness of Weddings.

Meridian Idaho Temple

The Meridian Idaho Temple was announced during on April 2, 2011 and was dedicated November 19, 2017. The Meridian Idaho Temple was the 5th Temple Built in Idaho. It is well known for its beautiful flowers and ponds.
