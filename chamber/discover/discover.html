The Creation of Westview, New Jersey

The greatest treasure that you will learn about Westview is that grief and sorrow is powerful enough to create magic and transform us into becoming who we are meant to be. Wanda Maximoff was pouring out her heart and letting out all her emotions from everything she lost in her life especially the sacrifice of a life together with Vision to save the universe when she magically transformed her grief into a whole town built upon the life she would have had with Vision. Wanda is the heart and soul of Westview and is a hero. She teaches that anyone can rise above their pain and transform it into joy. Letting go of what we have lost strengthens and allows us to grow. It sets us free.

Top 4 Interesting Facts about Westview

  1. The whole landscape of the town forms the shape of a hex. Westview has a hexagonal shape because it references the mindstone which also has a hexagonal shape. Wanda created Westview in this fashion because Vision was at the very center of her heart and soul. The love she has for Vision and everything that he taught her about hope and courge was poured into creating it after Vision.
  2. Westview's legondary city bird is a stork. It is common to see storks roaming around town. When you see one you can be sure that chaos is bound to happen followed by happiness. The stork is our legondary city bird because it symbolizes joy of a bright future ahead.
  3. Westview is home to beautiful mountains, gardens, and rivers. The top favorite destination to go to is the Wundagore Mountains. It is a peaceful place to go to meditate, fish, and go on hikes. It is the birthplace of Wanda becoming the Scarlet Witch and there is a museum exhibit that you can go to that showcases the journey of Wanda, the founder of Westview, becoming a hero as the Scarlet Witch.
  4. The town was named after 2 superheros Wanda and Vision. West refers to Wanda and View refers to Vision. Together they are Westview. View is symbolic to how Vision helps Wanda to keep into perspective of how her suffering and grief has the power to transform your whole view of life into something greator. West is symbolic to Wanda because she directs Vison towards becoming a hero.
